Privacy Policy

My Workouts respects your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

  • App usage data to improve performance and stability
  • Advertising identifiers used by third-party ad providers
  • Purchase information related to in-app purchases

Advertising

My Workouts displays ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use device identifiers to serve ads and measure performance.

In-App Purchases

My Workouts offers optional in-app purchases, such as removing ads. Purchase information is handled securely by Apple and is not stored directly by us.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only with trusted services required to operate the app (such as analytics or advertising providers).

Your Choices

You can manage ad tracking preferences in your device settings and choose whether to allow tracking when prompted.
